Areas outlined in red are designated by the FFIEC as eligible communities for the KeyBank Home Buyer Credit®. Eligible buyers may receive up to $5,000 toward closing costs and prepaid fees when financing a primary residence through KeyBank. Eligibility is based on the property's location and is subject to change. This is not a commitment to lend. All loans subject to credit and collateral approval.
